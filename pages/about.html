About Truppman Law

Dedicated legal professionals serving Fort Myers and Miami with integrity, experience, and results

Our Firm

Truppman Law has been serving the legal needs of individuals, families, and businesses throughout Florida for over three decades. Our commitment to excellence and personalized service has made us a trusted name in the legal community.

Based in Fort Myers with additional service in Miami, we understand the unique legal challenges facing Florida residents. Our comprehensive approach to legal representation ensures that our clients receive the attention and expertise they deserve.

We pride ourselves on our ability to handle complex legal matters while maintaining the personal touch that sets us apart from larger firms. Every client receives direct access to experienced attorneys who are committed to achieving the best possible outcomes.

Harold B. Klite Truppman

Attorney at Law

Harold B. Klite Truppman is a 1984 graduate of the University of Florida and a 1990 cum laude graduate of the University of Miami School of Law. While attending University of Miami, he also obtained a Masters Degree in Business Administration and completed the Graduate Program in Management Studies.

His practice consists of representing policyholders in evaluating, settling and litigating insurance claims, real estate, probate and other complex commercial litigation as well as corporate law.
